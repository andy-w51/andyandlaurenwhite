Andy & Lauren White

Seating Plan

Cornwall, UK - Lobster

After months of Andy telling Lauren all about how much she would love Cornwall, they finally took a week off and road tripped to the seaside in September 2015.

Andy drove Lauren all over the place on the small Cornish roads, and showed her some of the most beautiful places from his childhood like Padstow, Constantine Bay, St. Michael's Mount, and St. Ives.

"Paddy Lobstow", as he's lovingly called, is their souvenir from this great trip.
