Portland, OR - Beaver

Andy and Lauren journeyed to Portland, Oregon during their US trip in June 2015, to attend Lauren's cousin's wedding.

Andy did not realise quite how long it would take to drive through this state, as he claims everything in the US is actually much further away than it seems on a map. However, in a city known for craft beer and good food, Andy was in heaven.

The souvenir from this trip was a beaver, which is the mascot of the Oregon State University football team, which Lauren's cousin used to play for.
