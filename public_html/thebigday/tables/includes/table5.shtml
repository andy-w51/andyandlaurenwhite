Seattle, WA - Otter

Lauren and Andy visited Seattle in June 2015, whilst on a trip to the Pacific Northwest (aka the PNW), to attend Lauren's cousin's wedding in Portland, Oregon.

Along with Lauren's bridesmaid Deleena, they wandered around the city and relaxed by Puget Sound (on the lookout for otters) and enjoyed the beautiful scenery.

The souvenir from this trip was an otter, Deleena's favourite animal.
