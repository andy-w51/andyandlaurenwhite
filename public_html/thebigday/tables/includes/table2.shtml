Spain - Bull

Andy and Lauren went on one of their first "holidays", "vacations" together to Marbella and Sevilla, in the south of Spain in May 2014, with a group of Lauren's friends.

Andy walked miles along the beach in search of beach toys, and Lauren was pleasantly surprised when Andy came back with a volleyball...and then less excited when Andy just played football with it.

The souvenir from this trip was a bull from the old roman city of Ronda, up in the mountains.
